about me

Kamaljit Grewal is a developer who builds thoughtful digital experiences that combine airy, playful aesthetics with clean, functional code. She enjoys experimenting with new technologies and creating products that feel both intuitive and engaging. Her work spans roles as a software developer, web designer and developer, educator, and researcher across a range of fields, including medical imaging technology, cybersecurity, sustainable fashion, and environmental non-profit initiatives. Through this diverse experience, she brings a broad perspective on how technology can support meaningful and impactful work.

Kamaljit holds an Honours B.A. in Computer Science from York University.

Outside of work, she enjoys volunteering at the food bank, family movie nights, hikes, and solving zebra puzzles (puzzles can be found here).
